Banda Neira, Maluku


Banda Neira is located in the Banda Islands, which are part of the Maluku Islands in Indonesia. Specifically, it is situated in the Banda Sea, approximately 130 miles south of Ambon, the capital of Maluku Province. Banda Neira is known for its historical significance, picturesque landscapes, and vibrant marine life.

Uncover the secrets of Banda Neira's spice trade legacy as you stroll along its ancient forts and vibrant markets. Immerse yourself in the warm hospitality of the locals, savoring authentic Maluku cuisine. Bask in the tropical sun on pristine beaches, or embark on exhilarating hikes to witness panoramic island views.
