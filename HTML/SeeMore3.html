Raja Ampat, Papua Barat


Raja Ampat, an enchanting hidden paradise in Eastern Indonesia, has captivated the hearts of divers and nature enthusiasts alike. Renowned as the ultimate destination for those seeking the mesmerizing beauty of the underwater world, Raja Ampat boasts unparalleled marine wealth. With over 1,500 islands, majestic coral reefs, and captivating marine life, this destination entices adventurers with its captivating natural beauty.

Explore the diverse underwater biodiversity, ranging from vibrant coral reefs to breathtaking seagrass meadows. The splendor of Raja Ampat's natural panorama extends beyond the water's surface, encompassing lush green forests, stunning waterfalls, and untouched landscapes.
